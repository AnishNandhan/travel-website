Silent Andaman

Duration - 5D/4N


Set amidst beautiful coral reefs, Andaman Islands, allures visitors and wants them to never leave this place.
A plethora of activities can be enjoyed here like glass bottom boat rides, snorkeling, scuba diving, sea walking etc.
People from all over the world travel to North Bay Island to enjoy these activities.
For those who fear water, option of glass bottom ride is available for you wherein you can witness
the underwater world through glass panels enclosed in a boat.
If you would like to experience a bit of Seychelles in India, then head over to Radhanagar Beach without giving a second thought. This white sandy beach with coconut fringed coastline and crystal clear water is no less than a heaven.
Sunbathing and swimming are popular activities to try here. The beach comes under Havelock Island and can be reached by a boat or a ferry ride from Port Blair. This beach will provide a deep connectivity with nature and you will surely find solace being to the Radhanagar Beach.

 Highlights -

  • Enjoy some family time at the Elephant Beach
  • Get fascinated by the beauty of Radhanagar Beach
  • Enjoy light & sound show at Cellular Jail

 Itinerary -

Day 1 - Port Blair: Arrival and Sightseeing

Arrive at Port Blair, and transfer to the hotel. After settling in, take a tour of the Cellular Jail and attend the Light and Sound Show here.
Return to the hotel for the night stay.


Day 2 - Port Blair: Sightseeing

Today, after breakfast, you will be visiting Ross Island and North Bay Island. You will also get the chance to experience glass bottom boat ride and underwater corals.
AfterReturn to the hotel in Port Blair for the night stay.


Day 3 - Havelock Island: Arrival and sightseeing

After a lovely breakfast at the hotel, check out and transfer via ferry to the Havelock Island hotel. Check in and relax for a while. Enjoy your lunch at the hotel and post lunch explore Radhanagar Beach.
Refresh yourself by swimming and relax, before returning to your hotel on Havelock Island for the night stay.


Day 4 - Port Blair: A visit to Elelpant Beach

After breakfast, check out of the hotel and transfer to Elephant Beach, where you can enjoy plenty of water sports such as underwater walking,
snorkelling, banana boat riding and scuba diving. Later in the evening, you will be transferred to Port Blair for the night stay.
Check into your hotel and enjoy the evening shopping in Port Blair.
Return to the hotel for the night.


Day 5 - Departure

Prepare for your departure from the stunning Andaman and Nicobar Islands, we are sure that you had a great time here.
