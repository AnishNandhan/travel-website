The United Kingdom

Duration - 9D/8N


The United Kingdom, also called the U.K., consists of a group of islands off the northwest coast of Europe. It is a unique country made up of four nations: England, Wales, Scotland, and Northern Ireland. England, Wales, and Scotland also make up Great Britain.In northwest England and the Scottish Highlands are dozens of lakes, called lochs. These were left behind when the Ice Age glaciers melted. They tend to be long and narrow, and some are very deep. Legends say that a giant monster called Nessie lives in Loch Ness in Scotland.
Great Britain's rugged mountains, like the Scottish Highlands, offer habitat that is relatively untouched by humans. The country's 7,700 miles (12,429 kilometers) of shoreline, ranging from tall cliffs to beaches to marshes, also provide homes for wildlife such as seabirds and seals. From Land’s End in the south to John O’Groats in the north, the country is packed with tourist attractions that can fill a range of itineraries. With nearly 30 million tourists visiting every year, the UK is ranked among the top ten holiday destinations in the world. Known for its royalty, this country also represents a diverse patchwork of native and immigrant cultures

 Itinerary -

Day 1 - Arrival in London - Heathrow Apt

Your holiday would begin with arrival in London - a charming city with a royal heritage and incredible wealth of history. Rich in culture and exciting attractions, London has something to offer to every traveller - from history to fine food and good times. You would be greeted by a Travelsta representative at the airport and then proceed to the hotel.


Day 2 - London

Wake up to high levels of excitement as today you would be taken for a guided sightseeing of London. After breakfast, you would be picked-up from your hotel for a guided tour of the city. The major sights you would see during this tour include: Buckingham Palace - the office for the Head of State as well as the official residence of Queen Elizabeth ll, Piccadilly Circus, Trafalgar Square, Big Ben, Houses of Parliament, London Eye and others. Later Visit to Oxford street for some shopping. After spending a day in London and marvelling in its beauty, relish a scrumptious Indian dinner and enjoy a comfortable stay at the hotel.


Day 3 - London

After breakfast, you will be then taken to enjoy a ride atop the famous London Eye . The attraction is situated in the heart of London and from here you can get amazing panoramic views of the city, as well as sights such as: Westminster Bridge, Victoria Embankment and much more. Next you would head out to experience the glittering world of fame at one of London's most popular attractions, the Madame Tussauds Wax Museum.


Day 4 - Manchester

After a scrumptious breakfast, you would head to Manchester. En-route, you would be taken to Stratford - the quaint town which is known as the birthplace of the world-renowned play writer - William Shakespeare. Here you would get some time for shopping as well as a chance to explore the bars and public houses in one of the finest stone towns of England. After arrival in Manchester, get geared up for an orientation tour with photo-stops at some of the best known attractions of the city. You would drive past the Manchester Town Hall, where you can view the hall from outside and click some memorable pictures here. You would also stop to view the renowned Manchester United Football Stadium which is famous for hosting some nail-biting matches. Relish a scrumptious Indian dinner and then set off to your hotel to sleep.


Day 5 - Lanchester

After enjoying a delicious breakfast, check-out from the hotel as then you would proceed for a half-day excursion to Lake District. You would be taken to the quaint towns of Bowness-on-Windermere, which is the largest lake in England. Here you would enjoy a one way cruise around the mesmerizing islands of Lake Windermere which truly captures the essence of England.


Day 6 - Glasgow

After breakfast Head to Glasgow - which is a port city on the River Clyde in Scotland's western Lowlands. It's famed for its Victorian and art nouveau architecture, a rich legacy of the city's 18th–20th-century prosperity due to trade and shipbuilding. Today it's a national cultural hub, home to institutions including the Scottish Opera, Scottish Ballet and National Theatre of Scotland, as well as acclaimed museums and a thriving music scene.


Day 7 - Glasgow

A day at leasure OR you could opt for a full day excursion (additional cost) to Loch Lomond & Fort William. After breakfast you would head out for an excursion of Loch Lomond. A ‘loch’ is the Scottish name for a lake and Scotland consists of many lochs that add to its natural charm and beauty. In this excursion, you would get a chance to enjoy a scenic cruise in Loch Lomond. Later you will continue to Fort William and enjoy the Nevis Range gondola mountain experience.


Day 8 - Glasgow

Wake up to high levels of excitement as today you would be taken to Glenkinchie Distillery, the name might suggest, in a glen of the Kinchie Burn near the village of Pencaitland. It is situated about 15 miles from Edinburgh. The distillery is set in farmland. After seeing the Exibition area and having a taste of a singal malt, you will proceed to Edinburgh for a guided tour. On this tour, you would be seeing the Edinburgh Castle; the famous castle was built on the remains of an old volcano and is said to be haunted. Edinburgh has been the capital of Scotland since the 15th Century and stands proudly on seven hills creating the dramatic skyline of this lively cosmopolitan city. You can compare Edinburgh's Old Town to the elegant Georgian New Town with its three main streets and a square at either end. You will also see Castle rock. where you can hear the tales about the hangings that took place in the Grass market and the attempts to capture Edinburgh Castle as you marvel at the impenetrable Castle Rock. Later, return to Glassgow for dinner and comfortable overnight stay.


Day 9 - Departure from London - Heathrow Apt

After breakfast and check-out from the hotel. Agent drops you in airport and travelsta service ends here.
